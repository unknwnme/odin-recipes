Old Bay-Seasoned Shrimps

Description

Easy, spicy, steamed shrimp with Old Bay seasoning.

Great for a super-quick dinner with a salad.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup water
  • ½ cup white vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons seafood seasoning (such as Old Bay®)
  • 1 pound fresh largs shrimp, deveined with shells on

Steps

  1. Bring water, vinegar and seafood seasoning to a boil in a saucepan over high heat.
  2. Add shrimp and stir.
  3. Reduce heat to medium, cover and steam, stirring once or twice until bringt orange ni color. (3-5 Minutes)
  4. Drain & enjoy your juicy dinner.
