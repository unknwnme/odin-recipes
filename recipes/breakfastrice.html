Breakfast Rice

Description

This breakfast rice recipe is quick to make and delicious.

Adjust the amount of ingredients for your hunger needs!

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon vegetable oil
  • 1 cup cold cooked rice
  • 1 egg
  • 2 green onions, sliced
  • 2 teaspoons soy sauce

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Spread rice in a flat layer in hot oil; cook until crispy on the bottom. (2-3 minutes)
  3. Stir egg into rice until scrambled. (~2 minutes)
  4. Sprinkle green onions on top and season with soy sauce.
  5. Serve & enjoy your quick delicious asian breakfast.
